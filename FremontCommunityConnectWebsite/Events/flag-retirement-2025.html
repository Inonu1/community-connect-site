Flag Retirement Community Service

Date: July 2025

Location: Fremont neighborhood

Volunteers went door-to-door in the neighborhood to collect flags that needed to be retired. Each retired flag was disposed of in a respectful ceremony on the 4th of July. New flags were purchased from Costco to replace the collected ones and sold at the same price for record-keeping. Additional support was provided by installing flags for residents where needed.

The project combined civic service with community engagement, leaving residents with both a sense of tradition and renewed flags for their homes.
