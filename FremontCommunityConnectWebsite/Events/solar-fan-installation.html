Nature Learning Center Solar Fan Installation

Date: [Insert Date]

Location: Lake Elizabeth Nature Learning Center, Fremont

Volunteers researched, assembled, and installed a solar-powered insulation fan at the greenhouse to improve airflow and sustainability for the Nature Learning Center. The project combined hands-on work with environmental learning, demonstrating practical applications of renewable energy.

The activity provided insight into green technologies and their benefits for local community spaces. Students collaborated with staff to ensure proper installation and operation, contributing to both energy efficiency and educational value at the center.
